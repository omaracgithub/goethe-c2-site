Questions

Frequently asked

Is this affiliated with the Goethe-Institut?

No. GoethePrep is an independent study tool. Exam formats are modelled on official Goethe-Zertifikat structures, but we are not endorsed by the Goethe-Institut.

Which levels does the app cover?

All six Goethe exam levels: A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, and C2. This site focuses on B1 — the intermediate certificate required for German citizenship.

How long is the B1 exam?

About 2 hours 15 minutes for written parts plus 15 minutes for speaking.

How does the AI writing correction work?

You write answers freely in German. The app highlights grammar errors, suggests better vocabulary, and gives a score estimate.

What score do I need to pass?

At least 60 out of 100 points overall. Each module must be passed individually.

Is the app available on Android?

Currently iOS only (iPhone and iPad). No Android version planned.

Is it free?

First full exam is free. Subscription unlocks all 7 exams, drills, AI feedback, and stats.
