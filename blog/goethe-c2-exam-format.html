Goethe C2 Exam Format: Complete Guide

June 10, 2026·Goethe-Zertifikat C2

Key takeaways

  • Goethe-Zertifikat C2 certifies CEFR C2 (mastery).
  • Four skills: Lesen, Hören, Schreiben, Sprechen — 100 points total.
  • Typical duration: ~4 h written + ~15 min speaking.
  • This guide lives on c2.goetheprep.com — the C2 prep site.

Preparing for Goethe-Zertifikat C2? This guide on c2.goetheprep.com explains the official C2 structure. Academic teaching, translation, and demonstrating near-native competence.

What the C2 exam tests

  • Lesen: literary and abstract texts
  • Hören: extended authentic audio
  • Schreiben: nuanced formal composition
  • Sprechen: complex discussion and rhetoric

Scoring

Each skill is worth 25 points (100 total). You generally need ≥60 overall with minimums in Schreiben and Sprechen. See our passing score guide.
